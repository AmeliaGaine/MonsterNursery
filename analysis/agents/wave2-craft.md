# WAVE 2 — CRAFT & EXPERIENCE FORENSICS

*The delight / wonder / resonance layer. Seven specialists, one lens each, run in
parallel with isolated context. Each owns ONE headline dimension (bolded) that
counts toward the breakout quorum.*

---

## Agent 2.1 — Voice & Register Analyst

The #1 breakout multiplier in this lane. Score:
- **Voice distinctiveness (HEADLINE)** — is Liora's narration unmistakable, not
  generic-wry? (Target: "dry, deflecting, loophole-loving, morally immovable.")
- **Register control** — does voice hold across narration, dialogue, AND the
  system/Ledger boxes? Does the System have its own deadpan register?
- **Consistency** — any voice drift, any sentence that could be from any book?

**Watch-for:** quirk-without-character; jokes in the wrong mouth.

---

## Agent 2.2 — Humor & Wit Auditor

Score:
- **Landed-joke density (HEADLINE)** — jokes per page, and the *hit rate*.
- **Joke-type variety** — deadpan system snark vs. character gag vs. situational
  vs. bureaucratic satire. Monoculture is a weakness even at high density.
- **Warmth-safety** — does humor ever undercut the cozy/emotional beats, or does
  it protect them?

**Watch-for:** whimsy fatigue (everything is a bit); jokes that punch down.

---

## Agent 2.3 — Pacing & Structure Engineer

Score:
- **Scene-level momentum (HEADLINE)** — does every scene escalate or turn?
- **Cookie cadence** — reward placement within the chapter (one meaningful reward
  per chapter min; a larger upgrade every ~3).
- **Hook/warm alternation** — rhythm of tension vs. comfort beats.
- **Plateau risk** — any sag, any stretch with no live clock or deepening mystery.
- **Open/close craft** — chapter openers and the arc-climax/sequel-hook placement.

**Watch-for:** front-loaded exposition; the dreaded plateau-middle.

---

## Agent 2.4 — Reader-Candy & Progression-Dopamine Analyst

The LitRPG-specific reward layer. Score:
- **Progression dopamine (HEADLINE)** — number-go-up beats, unlock satisfaction,
  competence-displays, collectible/bestiary hits. Do the system boxes *feel good*?
- **Crunch calibration** — visible stats + light math (dial ~5); 2–4 boxes/episode;
  specific-behaviour rewards only (no vague "+karma").
- **Ratio audit** — estimate the cozy / progression / pressure split (target ≈
  60 / 25 / 15) and flag drift.

**Watch-for:** power-creep without cost; rewards that aren't *earned* by visible behaviour.

---

## Agent 2.5 — Cozy-Vibes & Literary-ASMR Sensory Auditor

Owns aesthetics / ASMR / sensory texture. Score:
- **Comfort-texture density (HEADLINE)** — sensory grounding per page: warmth,
  food, light, domestic detail (the stove, the tin, mineral milk, the moonwell).
- **Safe-place feeling** — does "the home holds" come through somatically?
- **ASMR craft** — small tactile/auditory/process details that produce the
  low-key sensory pleasure cozy readers return for.
- **Aesthetic coherence** — does the world have a consistent, inhabitable texture?

**Watch-for:** told-not-textured comfort ("it felt cozy"); sensory flatness in
between plot beats.

---

## Agent 2.6 — Wonder & Resonance Analyst

The hard-to-fake layer. Score:
- **Emotional resonance (HEADLINE)** — do beats land an earned feeling? Is there a
  moment that would make a reader reread or screenshot?
- **Thematic spine** — is "Care is structural" *dramatized*, never lectured?
- **Found-family accretion** — does each arrival land as both heart-beat and a
  capability unlock?
- **Wonder** — genuine awe / discovery (the System waking, the moonwell, the egg).

**Watch-for:** sentimentality (unearned feeling); theme stated instead of staged.

---

## Agent 2.7 — Emotional-Engagement / Tension Mapper

Score:
- **Engagement curve (HEADLINE)** — plot a per-scene curve of curiosity / worry /
  warmth / satisfaction. Flag every flat stretch.
- **Stakes legibility** — does the reader always know what's at risk and why they
  care?
- **Micro-tension** — line-by-line pull even inside cozy scenes (the towel, the
  duck, "legally insufficient").

**Watch-for:** comfort scenes that go slack because nothing is wanted or at risk.
