## FINDING 1 — Emotional monotone (M2) → **CLEARED**

The original charge was one register (dry) + one shape (negative→warm reframe ~15×), with
the palette only cosmetically auditable. The fix assigned each ep a primary color and told
the drafter to let the *interior* read as that color. Spot-checked ~10 across the arcs; the
colors are **genuinely written, not just tagged in the header.**

- **Ep2 (mischief):** interior runs comic delight — "fire-baby who will not be contained,"
  the duck-towel, "Do not agree with me. It encourages bad management." The closer is a
  mischief-closer (the towel re-singed, "finding a *third* towel"), explicitly NOT settle-in
  warmth (ep-02 l.6, l.76). Reads as mischief.
- **Ep4 (pride):** "the quiet craftsman's pride of a thing rebuilt well… a flue that draws…
  a spark *placed*"; the closer is "she made this, and it holds" — "I made a thing that
  works, and it works," held dry, explicitly distinguished from Ep3 wry-defeat and Ep5
  frustration→resolve (ep-04 l.8, l.62–64). Reads as pride, not a reframe.
- **Ep10 (melancholy):** "the ache under a win… the Tea Room's whole function is the goodbye";
  "the form did not have a box for how she felt about that"; the closer is "pride and loss in
  the same breath, the clock she didn't ask to start counting toward" (ep-10 l.6, l.51, l.70).
  Reads as melancholy.
- **Ep22 (mischief):** "three heads each certain the other got the better bowl; the gag-made-
  recipe… NOT a dry-reframe" — stated and executed; the alarm arms underneath but the surface
  is playful (ep-22 l.7). Reads as mischief.
- **Ep34 (grief):** "a name surfaces and it is erased blood; the interior is wound, not
  wonder-only… NO elegy." Metabolized as document + action ("She didn't cry. She copied the
  line… 'Rhea.'") — the drift bends **graver not warmer** ("the gravity IS the tenderness").
  This is the single clearest proof the fix changed the *shape*, not just the label
  (ep-34 l.3, l.42, l.53). Reads as grief.
- **Ep36 (dread):** "the documents turn the warm room cold; care was outlawed because it
  worked… the interior is the chill of a named, deliberate enemy, not warmth." The System
  "GOES COLD/QUIET" instead of warming; the cold is "behind her ribs, reported like a stat"
  (ep-36 l.2, l.39, l.41). Reads as dread — and is the literal inverse of the old warm-reframe.
- **Ep40 (grief):** "a voice that can almost cry is a thing that was once a person"; closer
  "grief-under-warm, NOT generic settle-in warmth" (ep-40 l.6). Reads as grief.
- **Ep45 (fierce-protect):** "she would burn the last of herself for the house and does";
  the claim made aloud, the jar emptied into the defense (ep-45 l.7). Reads as fierce-protect.
- **Ep46 (grief→peace):** moves from "a person worn flat by a generation of absence" to "the
  post kept, the handover finished"; Sorrel's grief sets the register (ep-46 l.6). Reads as
  the assigned arc.
- **Ep48 (quiet-pride):** "the title settles on a woman who already changed"; closer "the
  kettle, such as it was, was on," distinct from Ep47 catharsis / Ep49 hope (ep-48 l.6, l.62).
  Reads as quiet-pride.

**The old shape is genuinely broken in at least two places** (Ep36 dread = warm-room-goes-cold;
Ep34 grief = drift-goes-graver). The "negative→warm reframe" still exists where it belongs
(Ep2, Ep4 creature beats — that is the book's thesis), but it no longer monopolizes the
register: dread, grief, melancholy now read as themselves and the closers are varied per color
with no-repeat-within-2 honored in the headers. **This is a substantive fix, not a paint job.**

*Residual (MINOR, not re-opening):* the System still narrates much of the tenderness in
deadpan (RT4 §5's "tenderness-laundering" critique is untouched by M2 — M2 varied the *color*,
not *who carries it*). The colors are real; the delivery vehicle is still largely the appliance.
Out of scope for M2; noted so the praise is load-bearing.

## FINDING 2 — Liora plateau (M4) → **CLEARED**

The original charge: change early, then the "deflection drops, core stands" beat fires 3×
(Ep35/46/48), arc confirming not advancing. The fix: ONE seeded arc
(*loophole-finder who hides behind paperwork → advocate who claims the work aloud*), seeded
Ep3/5, stepped Ep17/24, **paid once Ep45**, with Ep46/48 explicitly de-duplicated. Verified:

- **Ep45 = the single payoff, intact.** She stops deflecting and claims the sanctuary OUT
  LOUD in her own voice: *"This is a sanctuary. I'm its keeper. You don't get to take it."*
  Headered as "the ONE Liora payoff — KEEP… not to be duplicated at 46/48" and rendered in
  S3 as "the woman in front of the form, not behind it" (ep-45 l.7, l.42).
- **Ep46 does NOT re-run it.** Header: "this episode does NOT re-run 'deflection drops, core
  stands' (that was Ep45's one payoff) — it is grief/recognition" (ep-46 l.6). The interior
  is meeting Edda, Sorrel confirming the voice, "Mm." decoded — **no re-claim of the
  sanctuary, no second speech.** Confirmed by reading S3 (ep-46 l.36–46): the beat is
  relationship/grief, not an arc-change repeat.
- **Ep48 is acceptance, a distinct beat.** Header: "ACCEPTANCE of the title… a CONSEQUENCE of
  the Ep45 change, not a re-run of it. No second speech, no re-claim" (ep-48 l.6). Rendered:
  "this beat is not the change happening again — it is the change having *consequences*… she
  simply *lets it be said about her* without flinching… 'A faded sign. / An empty slot. / A
  name that fit it.' / She did not write 'acting' in front of the word. She had run out of
  reasons to" (ep-48 l.43). This is acceptance, explicitly differentiated from transformation.

The arc is now **seeded → stepped → paid-once → consequence**, exactly as designed. The three-
times-confirmation pattern is gone. **CLEARED.**

*Note (the new under-motivation risk the task asked about — checked, NOT triggered):* M4 could
have left her change under-motivated if the seed/steps were only asserted. The headers cite the
seed at Ep3/5 (fear of being *seen*) and steps at Ep17/24; the supersession doctrine that the
Ep45 claim rides on is independently seeded Ep6/8/17 (ep-45 l.2). The change is load-bearing on
a real planted mechanic, not floated. The old RT4 §1 "no live 'go' option" (the stay/posting
clock has no leave-temptation) is **NOT addressed** by M4 and remains a MINOR — but it was a
MINOR in the original and is out of the MAJOR-fix scope.

## FINDING 4 — NEW issues introduced by the fix

- **[MINOR — NEW] Palette-vs-clock friction at Ep10.** The assigned color is *melancholy*,
  but Ep10's structural job is a number-up cookie (Trainee→Custodian I) landing on the turn —
  a candy/win beat. The scene works hard to hold both ("pride and loss in the same breath"),
  and largely succeeds, but the melancholy is doing tonal work *against* the rank-up dopamine
  the LitRPG structure wants there. It reads as managed rather than fully fused. Not a break —
  flagging it as the one place a color mildly fights its scene's mechanical purpose.
- **[MINOR — NEW] Sorrel's Ep34 gap is a fix-introduced inconsistency, not just an omission.**
  Because the fix-design *names* 34 and the surrounding eps (30/31/46) now establish Sorrel as
  the designated Edda-reader, her absence from the Rhea reveal is more conspicuous post-fix than
  it was pre-fix: the book has set up a character whose whole function is "reads the prior
  tenancy where Liora can't," then doesn't use her at the prior-custodian reveal. The fix
  half-created the expectation it doesn't pay. (Folded into Finding 3 PARTIAL.)
- **No color was found that actively *contradicts* its scene** (the task's worry). Ep36 dread,
  Ep34 grief, Ep45 fierce-protect all align with their plot turns. The Ep10 case is friction,
  not contradiction.
