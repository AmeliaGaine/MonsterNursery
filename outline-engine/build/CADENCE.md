*The per-chapter rhythm for all 49 episodes, scheduled against the IMMUTABLE
`ARCHITECTURE.md`. Honors CANON.md verbatim and GUARDRAILS G4 (cookie law), G5
(anti-plateau), G6 (no cliffhanger cruelty). Arc skeleton 12/12/13/12 = 49.*

> **How to read the columns**
> - **Cookie type** (G4): one of `number-up` · `mystery` (deepened/clue) · `relationship` ·
>   `joke` (landed) · `competence`. No two adjacent chapters repeat the same type.
> - **Out** (G6): `warm` (cozy floor — a synergy-pair beat from SM), `soft-hook` (a System
>   editorial note that implies the next problem), or `hard-hook` (a live clock advances —
>   never a cruel cliffhanger). Alternation: never two hard-hooks in a row; never three
>   warm-outs in a row.
> - **Live clock active** (G5): the live clock OR deepening-mystery confirmed running this
>   chapter. The System voice-drift tachometer (`L-EDDA`/`L-HEIR`) is on by construction in
>   *every* chapter, so this column is `YES` for all 49 — the named clock is the foreground one.
> - **Micro-structure** (3.3): `K&` = Kishōtenketsu (cozy/warm, satisfies without manufactured
>   conflict) · `S&S` = Scene&Sequel (tension). Target ~60% K& / ~40% S&S; every arc climax = S&S.
> - **Upgrade beat?**: a larger sanctuary/progression upgrade (room unlock, rank-up, skill,
>   loop-close that grows the map). Aligned to rooms-bible unlock episodes where sensible;
>   cadence target ~1 per 3 chapters.
