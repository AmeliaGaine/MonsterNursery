## Scene 2 — Tea with the candidate   [S2. Escalating want vs. obstacle]
- **Function / POV state:** The engine beat — give it the most room. WANT: run a matching interview as hospitality, not inspection, and have it *land* — prove the Tea Room mechanic by working it. - **Beats:**

The man warms. But the bar rises: warming, he leans in and *reaches* — to test the heat himself, hand toward a creature that runs hot when startled.
