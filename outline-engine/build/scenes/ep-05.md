- **Function / POV state:** Open in motion, mid-want, no scene-setting. WANT entering: get the Burrow Room sealed against the sleet *before* the cold undoes the morning's tending — a small, legible, physical job. The seam from Ep4 carries: snowmelt has turned to sleet, the Basking Nook holds heat the rest of the ruin still lacks, and Liora is chasing the one draught that won't quit.
- **Beats:**

  - Liora's reflex is the old one: clipboard, classification, *state your business.* She catches it. WANT hardens and complicates — she now needs two things that fight each other: finish the seal (habitat) and meet the world (the door now lets the world in).

## Scene 2 — The cull-office grammar, flipped   [S2. Escalating want vs. obstacle]
- **Function / POV state:** The engine beat — give it the most room. WANT: make a welcome out of a cull-clerk's vocabulary, in real time, with no desk and a freezing room behind her. The obstacle is *her own training*, and it answers back harder each try.
- **Beats:**
  - **Try 1:** The patron — a careful, unhurried sort scouting for a small warm creature for a shut-in relative — asks, reasonably, *what's the procedure.* Liora opens her mouth and Control comes out: name, jurisdiction, purpose-of-visit. The patron stiffens. She hears herself. First cost: the room has gone cold-formal, exactly the cold she's trying to un-build.
  - **Try 2:** She flips it — drags a crate to the threshold, sets the brass bell on it, turns the cull-intake questions inside out: not *state your business* but *who's it for, and what do they need to feel safe?* Better. But the obstacle raises the bar: the patron, warming, steps *inside* — and inside is the unsealed Burrow Room, the draught, the visible ruin. A nursery that can't hold its own heat doesn't read as one you'd trust a creature to.
  - **Try 3 (`[IDLE-TICK]` lands here):** Liora improvises a reception out of nothing — kettle on Cinder's safe heat, a chair that isn't broken, the guest book that is actually an old ledger turned to a clean page. Mid-pour the System posts the day's quiet number, unbothered: the world has now been *let in*, and the channel that opens can't be closed. Each fix costs effort she'd budgeted for the sill.
- **System box (idle-tick):** `> Renown channel: OPEN. Status revised: Stranger → Wary. Note: the door now lets the world in. This is the cost.`
- **Sensory / atmosphere:** Brass bell too bright for the bare crate; kettle-steam against sleet-grey glass; the draught still whistling one thin note behind every warm thing she sets down.
- **Lands here:** **Renown Stranger→Wary** (the idle-tick, in-register and on-page). Welcome-Desk function is being *built in the act* (formal unlock lands at the turn).
- **Exit:** The patron, half-won, glances past her at the cold room and the whistling gap — the warmth is real but the *structure* isn't proven. The whole visit hangs on whether the house can hold what it promises.

## Scene 3 — Mended, but the door is open now   [S3. The yes-but turn · COOKIE]
- **Function / POV state:** THE TURN. The cookie and the complication land in the same beat (yes-but). WANT met: prove the welcome is real by making the structure real — seal the room, *with the patron watching.*
- **Beats:**

Habitat Mending I comes online here — the competence-display — and it's rendered as method, not magic: read the draught to its source, set the wadding right, true the sill, call Bertram's keystone-instinct to settle the corner so the seal *holds* under load. The Burrow Room stops breathing cold. Hab climbs.
  - **The yes-but:** She gets it — the room is sealed, the patron is *sold* on the warmth and the competence both — **BUT** the very act of welcoming this stranger well has formalized the Welcome Desk, and a Welcome Desk means **arrivals she can't refuse.** The door that just earned her a patron is the door she can no longer shut. The win and the new standing obligation arrive together.
  - The patron, satisfied, asks to be put on the book for a spring match. Liora writes the first true entry in a guest ledger where the last book in this house listed creatures to be *removed.*
- **System box:** `> Habitat Mending I: registered. Burrow Room: sealed (Hab +). Welcome Desk: established. Note: intake is now reciprocal. You may no longer choose who needs you.`
- **Sensory / atmosphere:** The whistling note *stops* — the house goes a half-degree quieter; tallow-and-warm where there was cold; the ledger's clean page taking ink.
- **Lands here:** **🍪 competence (ON THE TURN)** · **Welcome Desk unlocks** · **Habitat Mending I online** · **Hab 12→20%** · **`~L0` advances** (a real patron, well-met, ticks the legitimacy clock toward viable). Door cracked one size larger.
