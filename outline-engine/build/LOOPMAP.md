# LOOPMAP.md — Stage 3.2 THREADED-LOOP WEAVER (the centerpiece)

*MICE + LIFO loop nesting for all 49 chapters of* The Monster Nursery, Book 1.
*Source of truth: `ARCHITECTURE.md` §2D (immutable C-graft ledger), `CANON.md`,
`GUARDRAILS.md` (G2/G4/G5/G6), `rooms-bible.md` (room-unlock loops),
`cast-synergy-map.md` (creature-bond loops). Arc skeleton 12/12/13/12 = 49 eps,
boundaries **Arc 1 = 1–12 · Arc 2 = 13–24 · Arc 3 = 25–37 · Arc 4 = 38–49**
(CANON skeleton wins over the rooms-bible's 25–36/37–49 working note).*

> **The principle being engineered:** a reader's felt sense of "tight plotting" is
> *literally* loops closing in the reverse order they opened (LIFO). Open A→B→C,
> close C→B→A. The deepest doll (opened last, inside the others) snaps shut first;
> the book shell closes last; exactly **one** door (`L-SERIES`) is left ajar.

---

## 0 — DEFINITIONS (so the audit is unambiguous)

- **MICE type** — **M**ilieu (place/exploration — locked East Wing, the building's
  hold, the larder), **I**nquiry (mystery — who wrote care into an outlawed System),
  **C**haracter (internal — culler→Matron, a creature learning to trust), **E**vent
  (external clock — inspection, send-off, alarm).
- **Nest depth** — **depth 0 = outermost shell** (opens first, closes last / never).
  Higher depth = inner doll (opens later, closes first). LIFO: for any nested pair,
  the higher-depth (inner) loop closes *before* the lower-depth (outer) one.
- **Spine loops (depth 0–2)** — the five standing drives that braid the whole book
  (`L-SERIES`, `L0`, `L-EDDA`, `L-LIORA`, `L-HEIR`). They are the book's *gravity*,
  always present in the background; they are **not** counted toward the 2–4 live-loop
  density target (that target governs the short-term braid the reader actively tracks).
- **Short-term loops (depth 3–5)** — the room-unlock and creature-bond loops that
  open, get worked, and close inside an arc or across two. These are what the density
  audit counts. A short-term loop is **live (foregrounded)** on a chapter where it has
  an on-page beat (open / progress-marker / close); between its open and its scheduled
  payoff it is *banked* — promised, paid attention to, but not crowding the foreground.
- **Pays cookie?** — per G4, every loop **close** lands ≥1 cookie
  {number-up · mystery clue · relationship beat · landed joke · competence-display};
  adjacent closes never repeat the same cookie type.

The depth-2 spine loops close in the final three chapters; the book shell (`L0`) closes
last; only `L-SERIES` (depth 0) remains open. **This is the "seal everything but one
door" discipline made literal.**

> **One LIFO grace note (logged, not a violation):** `L-CINDER` (d4, opened Ch4) closes
> its *answerable* half — the match — at Ch33, *after* `L-SPORE` (d4, opened Ch18) closes
> at Ch30. Two depth-4 siblings do not nest inside each other (LIFO governs *nested*
> pairs, outer↔inner, not same-depth peers), so peer ordering is free. Cinder's *send-off*
> is the deliberately deferred half (carried to Book 2 as ache-fuel, per ARCHITECTURE §2D
> note) — a clean **partial** payoff (G6), not an unpaid debt and not a second dangle: the
> pipeline itself closes on-page via Slime (Ch20) and Kettle (Ch41).
