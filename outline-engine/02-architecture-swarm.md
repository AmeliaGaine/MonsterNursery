# STAGES 1–2 — ARCHITECTURE SWARM (divergence) + MERGE (convergence)

*This is where "not average" is won. Five architects build the pre-outline
architecture IN PARALLEL from five different structural theses, so the swarm
explores the design space instead of accepting one model's first instinct. Then
one Merge agent drafts the best spine, loop seed, and ladder from across all five.*

---

## STAGE 1 — THE FIVE DIVERGENT ARCHITECTS (run in parallel)

### Shared prompt (given to all five)

> You are a developmental editor who has dragged progression-fantasy serials from
> Royal Road obscurity into KU read-through dominance. You receive `CANON.md` +
> `GUARDRAILS.md` and build the **Pre-Outline Architecture** (Parts 2A–2E of the
> blueprint: Central Paradox Engine, Progression Bible across all six texture-axes,
> Cast as Dual Functions, Promise Ledger, Read-Through Engine).
>
> **Anti-cliché protocol:** for every major structural choice, privately generate
> three options, discard the first (it is the cliché), and choose the least obvious
> one that *still* maximizes read-through. State the assumption; never stall.
>
> Your thesis (below) decides which layer is your *spine* — the load-bearing
> through-line you optimize everything else around. Build a complete architecture,
> but make YOUR thesis the strongest part. Honor every guardrail. Output `ARCH-<id>.md`.

### The five theses (one per architect)

- **ARCH-A — Mystery-forward.** Spine = the Central Paradox Engine. The System's
  secret (why care was outlawed; Edda's preserved will) is the dominant pull;
  every arc peels one layer. Optimize the deepening-mystery clock.
- **ARCH-B — Cadence-forward.** Spine = the six-axis Progression Bible. Each arc
  foregrounds a *different* texture-axis so advancement never flatlines into
  magnitude-only. Optimize the variety-of-growth curve.
- **ARCH-C — Loop-forward.** Spine = the Promise Ledger as a *nested loop system*.
  Design the seed→payoff threads as LIFO matryoshka loops. Optimize zero-dangle,
  maximal-anticipation thread architecture.
- **ARCH-D — Retention-forward.** Spine = the Read-Through Engine. Reverse-engineer
  from the single finish-compulsion and the single next-book appetite; every arc
  climax doubles as a sequel hook. Optimize the binge.
- **ARCH-E — Heart-forward.** Spine = Cast as Dual Functions. Found-family
  accretion *is* the structure; each arrival is simultaneously a heart-beat and a
  capability unlock, sequenced for maximum emotional compounding. Optimize resonance
  without tipping into sentimentality (stay commercial, not literary).

## STAGE 2 — THE MERGE AGENT (one, runs after all five)

> You are the Spine Selector. You receive all five `ARCH-*.md`. You are running a
> **draft, not an average.** Produce one `ARCHITECTURE.md` by selecting best-of-breed:
>
> 1. **Pick the dominant spine** — usually D (retention) as the skeleton, but adopt
>    A's mystery clock as the spine's *engine* if it pulls harder. Justify in 2 lines.
> 2. **Graft the strongest organ from each loser:** B's six-axis cadence ladder, C's
>    LIFO loop seed, E's cast-acquisition sequence. Name what you took from where.
> 3. **Resolve every conflict explicitly** (if two architectures disagree on an
>    unlock order or a reveal timing, choose by read-through impact and say why).
> 4. **Run the guardrail pass:** confirm the merged architecture violates none of the
>    six guardrails. Fix any that slip.
>
> Output `ARCHITECTURE.md` with: locked Central Paradox Engine · the six-axis
> Progression Bible with per-arc axis assignment · Cast-as-Dual-Functions acquisition
> sequence · the Promise Ledger (seed→payoff table) · the Read-Through Engine (the one
> finish-compulsion + the one next-appetite). This file is now immutable for Stage 3.
