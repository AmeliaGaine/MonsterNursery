# BRIARWICK ROOMS BIBLE — Book 1

*The base-building spine for* The Monster Nursery. *Every room is engineered to do
four things at once — a mechanical engine, a distinct sensory palette, an emotional/
thematic beat, and a threaded loop it opens or closes — so the base-building axis
reads as breakout reader-candy, never décor. Threaded onto the Outline Forge's
`LOOPMAP` / `CADENCE` / `STATSPINE` so rooms are scheduled, not merely catalogued.*

> **Canon source:** the two project docs + `manuscript/chapter-01.md`/`-02.md`.
> Canon rooms are marked *(canon)*. Episode numbers follow the Book-1 outline's
> 4-arc / 49-episode skeleton and are the *recommended unlock beats*, swappable.

---

## THE STRUCTURAL INSIGHT — two room economies

Briarwick does two jobs, so its rooms come in three flavors (this is what makes the
base-building feel richer than the genre average, and it builds in a recurring
emotional payoff the average book lacks: **the send-off**).

1. **Family wing (bespoke)** — the 5 core creatures are permanent; each gets a
   beloved, hand-restored habitat. The emotional spine.
2. **Boarding / placement** — the 4 fosters share a modular wing until matched to an
   adventurer patron; they do *not* get grand rooms (which makes the family rooms
   feel precious by contrast).
3. **Front-of-house & communal** — because the nursery runs *adoptions*, it needs
   patron-facing hospitality (Welcome Desk, Tea Room) that inverts the cull-office's
   coldness into warmth. This is the on-the-page differentiator.

Anti-power-creep rule honored throughout: **every room unlock introduces a new
upkeep/management problem** (a room is a liability as well as an asset).

## 1 — FAMILY HABITATS (bespoke)

### The Brew Pool *(beneath the Tea Room)* — Tea Kraken
- **Engine:** steeps & warms the house water; brews "mood teas" (buff meals). A
  tentacle surfaces in the communal pot to steal biscuits.
- **New problem:** mis-steeped → the whole house's tea (and morale) goes bitter.
- **Palette:** amber tea-light through steam · copper tub · bergamot & chamomile ·
  the slow deep *glug* of a contented kraken.
- **Loop:** opens the Tea-Room hospitality engine (Inquiry/Event support).
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 9 · **State:** Comfort +1, unlocks Resonance Cooking II input.

### The Three-Temper Kennel — Corehound
- **Engine:** tiny tri-personality guard-dog — one head alarms, one sulks, one has
  already forgiven you. Early-warning system.
- **New problem:** three heads, three thermostats (warm bed / cool tile / dark
  corner) that refuse to compromise — you must satisfy all three.
- **Palette:** hearthrug wool · dog-warm · three snores in three keys · small claws.
- **Loop:** comic-relief throughline; alarm payoff in the Arc-4 inspection.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 22 · **State:** Bond (Corehound) seeded; Habitat Stability +1.

### Moonwell Room *(canon)* — Lunar Mooncalf (Cress)
- **Engine:** reflected/borrowed lunar light; calming glow.
- **New problem:** cloud-nights starve it → ration moonlight.
- **Palette:** silver · cool still water · hush · porridge-and-rain.
- **Loop:** the first-trust proof (Character); recurs as the family's gathering pool.
- **Unlock:** **Ep 1 (canon)** · **State:** Cress 19%, Moonwell 12%→up, Comfort 1.

### The Pebble Garden — Cockatrice Chick
- **Engine:** failed petrification yields warm pebbles → a collectible *resource*
  (paths, habitat trim, gifts). A growing mosaic of its "failures."
- **New problem:** town prejudice (a "deadly" cockatrice) vs. the gentle reality —
  the prejudice-buster beat.
- **Palette:** sun-warmed stone · dry pebble-rattle · gentle-colored rock garden ·
  lizard-still heat.
- **Loop:** opens the town-fear/reputation thread; deflation-gag cookie engine.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 26 · **State:** Renown thread live; Bestiary +1; pebble resource.

### The Building Itself *(no fixed room)* — Blink Gargoyle (= Bertram)
- **Engine:** *load-bearing.* Content, it holds a wall as a keystone and blinks to
  where it's needed (security / "fast-travel").
- **New problem:** sulks → blinks off its post → a corner of Briarwick **sags**
  (Habitat Stability drops). Your brick has moods. Appears on the Welcome Desk at
  the worst moment. "Mm."
- **Palette:** cold granite gone warm where it sits · the soft *clop* of reappearing
  stone · dust-motes · a single slow blink.
- **Loop:** base-as-character throughline; Habitat-Stability mechanic anchor.
- **Unlock:** present from **Ep 1 (canon, as Bertram)**; mechanic formalized ~Ep 8.

## 2 — BOARDING / PLACEMENT (the four fosters)

### The Fledgling Wing *(modular alcoves)* — Phoenix · Slime · Shriekmoth
- **Engine:** one room, three micro-climates; a triage/socialization space.
  - **Pocket Phoenix** — anxious; bursts to a puff of ash and re-lights when
    startled. Alcove: soft warm ash-nest + thimble-brazier. *Arc: stop dying of nerves.*
  - **Puddle Slime** — leaves jewel-toned puddles that *dissolve grime* (useful) but
    slip-hazard (running gag). Alcove: tiled, gently draining.
  - **Shriekmoth (Kettle)** — screams at any light. Alcove: velvet dark. *Arc: a
    happy shriek that chimes, not screams.*
- **New problem:** three incompatible needs in one room; fosters cycle in/out.
- **Palette (three-in-one):** warm ash-grey · cool glossy drip-translucence · muffled
  velvet black.
- **Loop:** the placement pipeline (opens → each foster's send-off closes its loop).
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 13 · **State:** Boarding capacity +3; Bonds (Phoenix/Slime/Kettle) seeded.

### The Basking Nook *(canon)* — Ember-Toad (Cinder)
- **Engine:** thermal habitat — "heat is not harm when given somewhere safe to go."
- **New problem:** fire-risk in a wooden building if neglected.
- **Palette:** ember-glow · ash · dry radiant warmth.
- **Loop:** Cinder's bond → eventual match to a forge-adventurer (send-off).
- **Unlock:** **Ep 4 (canon)** · **State:** Basking Nook 30%→up, Comfort 2.

## 3 — FRONT-OF-HOUSE & COMMUNAL

### The Welcome Desk — entrance / reception
- The **anti-cull-office**: warm desk, kettle, guest book — bureaucracy inverted into
  hospitality the moment a patron walks in (the differentiator, on the page).
- **Palette:** worn oak · brass bell · lamplight · paper-and-tea.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 5 · **State:** patron intake enabled; Renown channel opens.

### The Tea Room — patron-meets-creature
- Adventurers don't shop; they take *tea* with a candidate (a cozy interview). Fed by
  the Brew Pool. Matches succeed over biscuits, not paperwork.
- **Palette:** mismatched china · low gold light · steam · the best chairs in the book.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 10 · **State:** Matching mechanic enabled; Comfort +1.

### The Resonance Hearth — care-cooking
- The mineral-milk ASMR scene made a room; buff-meals on a warmed silver spoon.
- **Palette:** steam · spoon-chime · porridge · the stove with opinions.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 11 (formalizes canon kitchen) · **State:** Resonance Cooking II.

### The Lull — communal sleep
- Found-family made architectural: predator and prey piled together asleep = the
  thesis, literal (all three corehound heads finally agree on one thing — nap).
- **Palette:** breathing · low warm dark · wool and feather.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 28 (Arc-3 peak) · **State:** peer-comfort buff; Comfort +2.

### The Mending Room — infirmary (Dr. Sorrel)
- Heals creatures *and* carers; quietly fixes Liora too ("Briarwick rescues people").
- **Palette:** clean cool light · herb-bunches · linen · eucalyptus calm.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 16 · **State:** Diagnosis II / treatment unlocks; Dr. Sorrel joins.

### The Book Nook — story-hour reading corner
- A window-seat reading corner: quilts, lamplight, a shelf of the nursery's old
  records-turned-storybooks, rain on the glass. The cozy-ASMR keystone (so the Lull
  isn't carrying it alone).
- **Engine:** nightly *story hour* gentles the scariest creatures (Cockatrice,
  Shriekmoth) by being read to — the Soothing Song engine.
- **Palette:** soft wool · warm low lamp · rain-on-glass · paper.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep23 · **State:** Soothing Song support; Comfort +1. *(See `cozy-sensory-bible.md`.)*

### The Send-Off Porch — placement payoff
- The bittersweet dopamine beat the genre average lacks: a raised creature leaves with
  the right patron. Resolve + ache + a new appetite (who arrives next?).
- **Palette:** dawn light · a packed basket · the road · a held breath.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 20 (first send-off) · **State:** first patron match closes a foster loop.

## 4 — UTILITY / MYSTERY

### The Archive (Ledger Room)
- Base-building fuses with the mystery — restoring the room *is* unlocking Edda's
  secret. Exploring the base advances the plot.
- **Palette:** dust · dry paper · the redacted line.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 31 · **State:** lore axis steps; "Previous Custodian" thread advances.

### West Wing larder *(canon)* — living sugar-spore lichen
- A pantry that's also an organism; **sporeglow** feed/heal resource.
- **New problem:** spreads if unmanaged.
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 18 (canon hinge) · **State:** sporeglow resource; mystery goes live.

### East Wing *(canon)* — the locked finale
- The forbidden room held for the climax. Occupant currently **[OPEN]** (the dropped
  dragon slot is yours to fill or keep dark).
- **Unlock:** ~Ep 30 (opened) · **State:** finale space; Habitat map completes.
